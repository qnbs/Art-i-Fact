# Art-i-Fact: Your Personal AI Art Curator

**Art-i-Fact** is an innovative web application that empowers you to discover, create, organize, and share virtual art galleries using state-of-the-art AI curation tools. It acts as your intelligent assistant, blending your unique curatorial vision with the vast knowledge of AI to craft beautiful and insightful art experiences.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features

-   **Discover Art:** Explore a vast universe of art. Simply enter themes, styles, emotions, or even colors, and let the AI curate a collection of relevant masterpieces for you. You can also upload an image or use your camera to find visually similar artworks.
-   **AI Studio:** Become an artist! Generate unique, original artworks from your text descriptions using a powerful image generation model. You can also **Remix** existing AI art with new prompts for iterative creation.
-   **Curate Virtual Galleries:** Build and organize your own galleries. Add artworks, give your gallery a compelling title and description, and arrange the pieces with drag-and-drop to tell a story.
-   **AI Assistant:** Enhance your curations with a suite of intelligent tools:
    -   **Suggestions:** Get suggestions for new artworks to complement your collection.
    -   **Text Generation:** Generate evocative titles, descriptions, and a professional curator's introduction.
    -   **Smart Reorder:** Let the AI suggest the optimal order for your artworks to create the best narrative flow.
    -   **Critique:** Receive a professional, constructive critique of your gallery.
    -   **Audio Guide:** Create a complete, AI-narrated audio guide for your exhibition.
    -   **Cinematic Trailer:** Generate a short, atmospheric video trailer for your gallery to share.
-   **Curator's Journal:** Keep a personal journal to jot down your thoughts and research. Link entries to specific galleries and use AI writing tools to expand, summarize, improve, or research selected text.
-   **Command Palette:** Access all major functions, navigate quickly, and search through all your content (galleries, journals, artworks) from anywhere with a simple `Ctrl+K` command.
-   **Exhibit & Share:** Present your finished galleries in a beautiful, immersive exhibition mode. Share your creations with the world via a simple link for viewing or as a template for others to use.
-   **Personalization:** Create a curator profile, track your stats, and customize the app's appearance and behavior in the settings.

## üöÄ Getting Started

The workflow is simple and intuitive:

1.  **Discover or Create:** Use the **Discover** tab to find existing artworks based on your ideas, or visit the **Studio** to generate your own.
2.  **Add to Gallery:** Click on any artwork you like to add it to your current gallery. If you don't have one, a new one will be created for you.
3.  **Curate & Enhance:** Go to the **Workspace** tab to manage your projects and galleries. Select a gallery to edit the title, description, and reorder the art. Use the **AI Assistant** ‚ú® to get suggestions, generate text, create an audio guide, and more.
4.  **Exhibit & Share:** Once you're happy with your gallery, click "Exhibit" to view it in an immersive slideshow mode or "Share" to get a link to send to others.

## üé® AI Studio Explained

The AI Studio is your personal canvas for digital art creation. Powered by Google's advanced models, it transforms your textual descriptions into stunning, high-quality images.

-   **How it works:** Simply type a descriptive prompt of what you want to see. The more detailed your description, the more nuanced the result will be. Use the "Enhance Prompt" feature to let the AI help you write a more effective prompt.
-   **Remixing:** After generating an image, you can enter "Remix" mode. This allows you to provide additional prompts (e.g., "add a full moon," "make it winter") to modify and refine the image iteratively.
-   **Experimentation:** Play with different styles ("oil painting," "photorealistic," "watercolor"), lighting ("cinematic lighting," "soft light"), and compositions.
